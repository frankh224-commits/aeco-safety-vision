# Governance Checklist

## Privacy and Consent
Images used in this project do not contain personal identifiable information.

## Data Minimization
Only images necessary for detecting helmets and safety vests were used.

## Intended Use
The model is intended for research and educational purposes.

## Limitations
The dataset is small and the model may produce false positives or false negatives.

## Risk Note
False negatives may cause safety equipment violations to go undetected.
